Structurely · Pricing Model Flow

Three components at activation: Implementation Fee, Support Plan, and Credits.
Credits are usage-based — the AI draws from your credit balance as it works. At ~90% usage, sales reaches out to discuss next steps.
At 100%, the AI mutes automatically — campaigns keep running, but the AI won't respond until the account is replenished or the client agrees to continue.

Step 1 · Sales
Scope the Plan Before Signing
Establish the client's monthly budget, lead volume, and the tasks/processes AI will automate. Present the right support plan tier and a credit amount that maps to their goals — sell why the proposed volume makes sense for what they're trying to accomplish.
Step 2 · Activation
What the Client Gets — and When They Pay
Implementation Fee (Month 0, one-time) — CRM integration, agent build, onboarding support, fine-tuning, and training. Standard $1,500 · Enterprise $2,500. Includes 10 hours of service in Month 0.

Support Plan (kicks in Month 1) — ongoing account management, agent monitoring, and optimization. $499/mo · 4 hrs  |  $999/mo · 8 hrs.

Credits (Month 1 onward) — the AI's working fuel. 1 SMS = 1 credit · 10 sec of voice = 1 credit. Client commits to a minimum monthly credit volume upfront; extra usage beyond that is billed daily at the same rate. No rollover.

Credit Pricing · Minimum Monthly Commitment
Client agrees to a minimum monthly lead volume. That credit block is charged upfront at the start of each cycle. Extra usage beyond the minimum is billed daily at the same rate — no penalty.

Watch Credit Usage Against Credit Limit
Monitor credit usage so you can see who is approaching their credit limit and ensure proactive outreach before it's reached. Important: campaigns continue running even after the credit limit is hit — clients should stay on top of how many leads they're enrolling.

Option A
Pause Until Next Cycle
AI Mutes at 100% — Campaign Keeps Running
Once credits hit 100%, the AI mutes automatically. Campaigns continue running — but the AI won't respond to active conversations until the next billing cycle begins. No additional charges.
Option B
Continue with Auto-Pay
Client Agrees to Continue Past the Credit Limit
Client opts in to continue past 100%. Daily usage is calculated each day and charged in Stripe the following day at the same per-credit rate — no penalty. Missing a payment triggers an immediate AI mute.
⚠ Non-Payment (Option B)
AI Mutes Until Balance Is Cleared
Unpaid daily usage mutes the AI immediately. Campaigns continue running — but the AI won't respond to any active conversations until the balance is paid and the account is reinstated.
Step 6 · Cycle Repeats
New Billing Cycle Begins — Reset & Review
If a client is consistently at or above their credit limit, sales should work with them to increase their monthly credit volume — moving to a higher band also unlocks a lower per-credit rate.

Note: Clients cannot adjust their support plan or credit volume themselves — all changes go through sales/CS.

Important · How Money Moves
At Activation & Through the Pilot

Month 0: Implementation fee only — $1,500 standard, $2,500 enterprise. Includes 10 hours of service. Clock starts here.

Month 1–2 (Pilot): Support plan begins + committed pilot credits charged at cycle start. Extra usage billed daily at the same rate by Serenity. 60-day pilot from go-live — client can renegotiate credit commitment or opt out before Month 3.

Month 3+: Converts to 12-month contract. Support and minimum monthly credit block renew each cycle. Extra usage continues daily.

If Credit Usage Hits 100% Mid-Cycle

Option A — Pause: AI mutes automatically. Campaigns keep running but AI won't respond. Resets at the next billing cycle. No additional charges.

Option B — Continue: Client opts in. Extra usage charged daily by Serenity at the same per-credit rate as their committed tier — no penalty pricing. Unpaid daily usage mutes the AI until cleared.

Capture credit volume, support tier, and daily usage terms in proposals so expectations are set before signing.
