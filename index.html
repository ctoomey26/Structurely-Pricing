Structurely · Pricing Model Flow

Three components at activation: Implementation Fee, Support Plan, and Credits.

Credits are usage-based — the AI draws from your credit balance as it works. At ~90% usage, sales reaches out to discuss next steps. At 100%, the AI mutes automatically — campaigns keep running, but the AI won't respond until the account is replenished or the client agrees to continue.

Step 1 · Sales
Scope the Plan Before Signing
Sales establishes the client's monthly budget, lead volume, and the tasks/processes AI will automate. From there: present the right support plan tier and a credit amount that maps to their goals — sell why the proposed volume makes sense for what they're trying to accomplish.
Step 2 · Activation
Implementation Fee + Support Plan + Credits
Implementation Fee — covers CRM integration, agent build, 30-day onboarding support, fine-tuning, and training.

Support Plan — ongoing account management, agent monitoring and optimization. $499/mo (4 hrs) or $999/mo (8 hrs).

Credits — the AI's working fuel. Credits are consumed as the AI engages leads. Replenish monthly; rate is based on your volume tier.

Step 3 · Usage Monitoring
Watch Credit Usage Against Credit Limit
Monitor credit usage so you can see who is approaching their credit limit and ensure proactive outreach before it's reached. Note: it's important clients stay on top of how many leads they're enrolling in campaigns — campaigns continue running even after the credit limit is hit.

Option A
Pause Until Next Cycle
AI Mutes at 100% — Campaign Keeps Running
Once credit usage hits 100%, the AI mutes automatically. Campaigns continue running — but the AI won't respond to active conversations until the next billing cycle begins. No overage charges.
Option B
Continue with Auto-Pay
Client Agrees to Continue Past the Credit Limit
Client opts in to continue past 100%. Overage is calculated per day; each day's overage is charged in Stripe the following day via auto-pay. Missing a payment triggers an immediate account pause.
⚠ Non-Payment (Option B)
AI Mutes Until Balance Is Cleared
Unpaid overage amounts mute the AI immediately. Campaigns continue running — but the AI won't respond to any active conversations until the balance is paid and the account is reinstated.
Step 6 · Cycle Repeats
New Billing Cycle Begins — Reset & Review
If a client is consistently at or above their credit limit, sales should work with them to increase their monthly credit volume — moving to a higher band also unlocks a lower per-credit rate.

Note: Clients cannot adjust their support plan tier or credit volume themselves; all changes go through sales/CS.

Important · How Money Moves
At Activation

Client is charged three components: Implementation Fee (integration, agent build, onboarding support), Support Plan ($499/4 hrs or $999/8 hrs — account management & monitoring), and Credits (usage-based, rate depends on lead volume tier). Support plan and credits renew on the same rolling cycle anchored to the activation date.

If Credit Usage Hits 100% Mid-Cycle

Option A — Pause: AI mutes automatically. Campaigns keep running but the AI won't respond. Resets at the next billing cycle. No additional charges.

Option B — Auto-Pay: Client opts in to continue. Overage is calculated each day and charged in Stripe the following day. Unpaid overage mutes the AI until the balance is cleared — campaigns continue running.

Capture credit volume and overage rules in proposals so expectations are clear before signing.
